Steamed Eggs

Description

A timeless dish perfect for any day of the time, whenever, wherever.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oyster sauce
  • Chives
  • Minced meat (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk eggs.
  2. Mix minced meat with oyster sauce, and place in a bowl.
  3. Pour whisked eggs into bowl with minced meat.
  4. Steam for 10 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle chives once cooked.
