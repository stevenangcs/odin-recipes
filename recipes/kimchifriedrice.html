Kimchi Fried Rice

Description of Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe:

Kimchi Fried Rice is a simple dish that everyone can make within a few minutes. Easy, delicious, and it packs a punch!

Ingredients:

To make a basic Kimchi Fried Rice, all you need is:

  • Rice (Depends on how much you need)
  • 50 grams of kimchi
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Seaweed flakes
