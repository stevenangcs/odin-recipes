Kimchi Fried Rice

Description of Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe:

Kimchi Fried Rice is a simple dish that everyone can make within a few minutes. Easy, delicious, and it packs a punch!

Ingredients:

To make a basic Kimchi Fried Rice, all you need is:

  • Rice (Depends on how much you need)
  • 50 grams of kimchi
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Seaweed flakes

There are also a few optional ingredients that you may like to put into your kimchi fried rice, such as:

  • Protein - Beef, Chicken or Pork is fine.
  • Spam
  • Sausages

Steps

  1. Place the rice onto the pan.
  2. Pour the kimchi over the rice. You want to ensure that the kimchi sauce is adequate.
  3. Taste to see if the rice is salty enough. Depending on preference, add one tablespoon of soy sauce and taste. Repeat as necessary.
  4. Crack the egg in a separate bowl and whisk briefly. Once stirred, pour egg into the fried rice and mix thoroughly.
  5. Once egg is cooked, place the kimchi fried rice into a plate and sprinkle the seaweed flakes on top.

You are now ready to eat the kimchi fried rice!
