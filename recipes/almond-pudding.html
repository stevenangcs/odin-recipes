Almond Pudding

Description of Almond Pudding Recipe:

Sweet and silky almond pudding is always a nice dessert to close a hearty meal.

Ingredients:

Surprisingly little ingredients are needed for a basic almond pudding!

  • Almond Pudding Agar Powder
  • Chopped / canned fruits (mangoes, strawberries)
  • 350ml of water

Steps

  1. Pour all the water into a pan and heat it up for 3 minutes
  2. Pour almond pudding agar powder into the water. Stir gently for 7 minutes.
  3. Once 7 minutes are up, pour the almond pudding mixture into your preferred container. Let the mixture rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours.
  5. When ready to eat, serve with chopped fruits for a more refreshing palette.
