Spaghetti Bolognese

Description of Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe:

This spaghetti bolognese recipe is one that has an extra chunky sauce!

Ingredients:

  • 100g of minced beef
  • 50g of chopped champignon
  • 25g of diced onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 jar of pre-made spaghetti tomato paste
  • 200g of spaghetti pasta
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour the entire jar of pre-made tomato paste into a pan and heat it up.
  2. Place all the minced beef, chopped champignon and onions into the pan. Let it simmer for 1 hour.
  3. Boil 300ml of water and sprinkle a pinch of salt in the water
  4. Once water is boiling, place spaghetti pasta into the boiling water and wait for 10 minutes.
  5. Seive out water and thoroughly dry the pasta.
  6. Place spaghetti pasta on a plate, and pour as much of the spaghetti sauce.
  7. If you have leftover spaghetti sauce, you can refrigerate it!
